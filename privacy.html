This Privacy Policy explains how Ghana Trotro Transit ("GTT", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Android app, web app, and website (together, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who We Are

Ghana Trotro Transit is built and operated by NxNx Tech, CC416 Adjei Kwashi Street, Accra, Ghana. GTT is an independently developed transit-information tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by GPRTU, any government agency, or any trotro operator.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Feedback and bug reports: When you use the feedback form, you may provide your name, a message, and a feedback category (bug report, feature request, route correction, compliment, or general feedback). Submitting feedback opens WhatsApp and sends your message, along with your WhatsApp phone number, directly to our support line.
  • Custom or saved routes: If you create, save, or share a custom route, we store the route details (origin, destination, stops) you enter.

2.2 Location Data

Location access is optional and only used when you enable it. If granted, we use your device's GPS coordinates to show nearby trotro stations, calculate route suggestions, and provide live navigation prompts and stop alerts. Location data is processed on-device or transmitted only as needed to generate route results, and is not compiled into a persistent history of your movements or sold to third parties.

2.3 Usage and Device Data

We automatically collect limited technical data to keep the Service working reliably, including:

  • Device model, operating system and version (Android, iOS, or browser/OS for the web app)
  • App version (e.g. Beta 4)
  • General usage data such as routes searched, screens viewed, and crash/error logs
  • IP address and network-level information when using the web app

2.4 Offline and Local Storage

To support offline navigation, the app caches route maps, saved routes, and favourites locally on your device in sandboxed app storage. This cached data stays on your device and is used to let you view routes without a data connection.

3. How We Use Information

  • To find and display trotro routes, stops, and fare estimates
  • To provide real-time guidance, stop notifications, and offline route access
  • To sync your saved routes and favourites across devices where you use the same account/session
  • To respond to feedback, bug reports, and route corrections
  • To improve route accuracy using aggregated, community-contributed route data
  • To maintain app stability and diagnose technical issues

4. Fare and Route Data Sources

Fare estimates referenced as "GPRTU-approved" are based on publicly available fare information and are provided for guidance only. Actual fares, routes, and schedules may vary due to traffic, road conditions, fuel prices, or individual driver/operator decisions, and are not guaranteed to be accurate at the time of your trip.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information in the following circumstances:

  • Feedback via WhatsApp: messages sent through our feedback form are transmitted via WhatsApp/Meta's messaging infrastructure and are subject to WhatsApp's own privacy practices.
  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, or crash-reporting providers that help us operate the Service, bound to use data only for that purpose.
  • Legal requirements: where required to comply with Ghanaian law, respond to lawful requests, or protect the rights, safety, or property of GTT or its users.

6. Mobile App Permissions

  • Location (optional): used only for route search, nearby stations, and live navigation, and only after you grant permission via your device settings.
  • Push notifications: used for stop alerts, route updates, and service announcements. You can disable these at any time in your device settings.
  • Local storage: used to cache maps and routes for offline use, kept in an encrypted, sandboxed app folder inaccessible to other apps.
  • Internet connection: required to fetch live route and fare data; the app falls back to cached offline data when unavailable.

You can review or revoke any of these permissions at any time through your device's operating system privacy settings.

7. Cookies (Web App)

The web version of GTT may use cookies or similar local storage technologies to remember your preferences and saved routes within your browser. You can clear or block cookies through your browser settings, though this may limit some features.

8. Data Retention

We retain feedback and usage data only as long as necessary to operate and improve the Service, respond to your requests, and comply with legal obligations. Locally cached offline data remains on your device until you clear the app's storage or uninstall the app.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

11. Your Rights and Choices

  • You may decline location, notification, or storage permissions at any time via your device settings.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have provided to us (e.g. feedback submissions) by contacting us at the details below.
  • You may stop using the Service and uninstall the app at any time, which removes locally cached data from your device.

12. Third-Party Links

The Service may link to third-party sites (such as our web app, social media, or app stores). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites, and we encourage you to review their policies separately.
