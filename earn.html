How to Earn

Four simple steps from sign-up to Mobile Money in your pocket.

Download the app & create an account

Get Ghana Trotro Transit on Android, sign up with your name, email, and phone number. That's your contributor profile, all your approved submissions and earnings are tracked here.

Tap on the profile button, scroll to bottom and click on Earnings. Fill out the form and a unique Earing ID will sent to your phone you used when signing up on the app.

Free to join

Add stops and routes you know

Visit the website sent together with your Earnings ID. Pin stops on the live map, enter the stop name and GPS location, then build a route by connecting stops in order and setting the fare for each segment. You can also bulk-upload stops from an Excel or CSV file if you know a lot of locations.

Fill in as many details as you can, vehicle type, operating hours, frequency, and amenities all increase the chance of fast approval.

Works anywhere in Ghana

Submit for review

To prevent spam and protect data quality, admins will review your submission within 24 hours. If there's any fault with your submission, you'll be notified and given the chance to make corrections.

Reviewed within 24 hrs

Get approved & receive your payment

The GTT admin team checks every submission for accuracy, correct stop positions, proper fare ranges, and real operating data. Once you get 100 approved stops and 50 approved routes, your earning will be sent directly to your registered Mobile Money number. The more stops and routes you add, the more you earn.

Paid to your MoMo automatically
What you contribute

Stops & Routes — Both Pay

Every piece of data you add helps commuters and earns you money. Here's exactly what you can submit.

Trotro Stops

A stop is a single location where trotros pick up or drop off passengers, a junction, a market, a roadside landmark. Every approved stop earns you GH₵ 0.2+.

  • Tap to pin on the live map
  • Type coordinates manually (lat/lng)
  • Bulk upload via Excel or CSV
  • Auto-finder scans your region for known transit spots
  • Preview on map before saving

Trotro Routes

A route connects stops in the order a trotro actually travels — from one end of town to another. Each approved route earns you GH₵ 0.6+.

  • Select stops in travel order
  • Set the fare for each stop segment
  • Choose the vehicle type (Trotro, STC, Metro Mass…)
  • Add operating hours and departure frequency
  • Note amenities (AC, charging ports, luggage space)

The more detail you provide when submitting a route, the faster it gets approved. These are the fields you can fill:
