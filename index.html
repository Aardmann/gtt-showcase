The Problem

Getting Around Ghana
Shouldn't Be This Hard

Millions of Ghanaians rely on trotros daily, yet face persistent challenges that make commuting stressful and inefficient.

Beyond Google Maps

Google shows direct routes A to B. Our app provides detailed trotro routes with all intermediate stops, stations, and junctions specific to Ghana's transit system.

Never Get Stranded

With real-time guidance, stop notifications, and route tracking, you will always know exactly where to go — no more asking strangers or getting lost.

Transparent Fares

Get real-time fare updates directly from GPRTU. Know the exact fare before boarding, avoid conflicts, and plan your budget accurately.
